About TLI

Tesla Learning Institute(TLI) has been envisioned as a learning platform where we are reinventing education. In today's competitive world, everyone wants to be a step ahead of others which results in anxiety, depression and other mental issues. If only learning could be fun instead of being a race, we will achieve things we never even imagined possible. Education is empowerment. It empowers you with tools to make your dreams a reality. We at TLI, are providing these tools to school students.

About Courses

This session the courses we are offering are majoring can be categorised into two: Main Courses, Power Courses. Main Courses are designed to cater to academics and cover CBSEandMPBSE curriculum for classes from VI to XII in India. These courses are specially designed to make learning fun and not a burden that you need to carry in your head. Power courses have been designed to develop the capabilities of an individual. Like Memory, Techniques course helps the student in memorizing with ease and for a longer duration. English course empowers students with the ability to express in their own way and helps immensely in subjects like science and social science. Yoga course enhances their physical and mental capabilities. Many more courses are available. Please visit our course page for more details.
